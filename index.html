Kelly Zhou is a web developer and multimedia journalist based in Orange County, Calif. She graduated from UCLA in 2013 and has since worked as a reporter at the Orange County Register, a regional news publication. She has previously worked at the Participant Media, the UCLA Daily Bruin, UCLA Magazine, 23andMe and more. She is a programming nerd, running enthusiast and journalist who appreciates telling people's stories. Interested in working together? Send an email.
