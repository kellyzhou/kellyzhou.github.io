Kelly Zhou is a web developer, journalist and multimedia enthusiast. She codes in PHP, HTML5, CSS3, MySQL, and JavaScript daily. Kelly has strong experience in responsive frameworks (such as Bootstrap), wireframing and design, best practices in SEO, Mailchimp email campaigns, Google Analytics, Google Tag Manager and DoubleClick Manager. Kelly graduated from UCLA in 2013 and previously worked as a reporter at the Orange County Register, a regional news publication with 2.7 million unique monthly visitors. She has previously worked at Participant Media, the UCLA Daily Bruin, UCLA Magazine, 23andMe and more. She is a programming nerd, running enthusiast and journalist who appreciates discovering people's stories. Interested in working together? Send an email.
